Gitk App
The upper left pane shows series of commits to this repository, with the most recent on top.
The pane to its immediate right is the commit author and its far right, the timestamp.

Its midsection contains the SHA ID, as well as other tools that may help us with finding
specific commits.

The bottom left will give us a diff file of all the changes made in this commit 
as well as give us more details on this commit section.

In our last section, the bottom right, it will show the various files affected by the
given commit.
